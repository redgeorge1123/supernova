Flappy Bird: Unblocked Game

Flappy Bird is a side-scrolling browser game where players control a small bird by tapping the screen to make it "flap" and fly. The objective is to navigate through gaps between green pipes without crashing. It's known for its simple controls but challenging gameplay, encouraging high-score competition.

How to play Flappy Bird?

  • Start the Game

    Open the flappybird.gg website on your device and press "Start" to initiate gameplay.

  • Tap to Fly

    Tap the screen to make the bird flap its wings, allowing it to fly upwards. Releasing your tap makes the bird descend due to gravity.

  • Navigate Pipes

    Keep the bird afloat by tapping to fly through the gaps between green pipes that approach from the right.

  • Avoid Obstacles

    Ensure the bird doesn't hit any pipes or the ground, as crashing ends the game.

  • Strive for a High Score

    Repeat the process to improve your skills and try to surpass your previous high score.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flappy Bird?

Flappy Bird is a mobile game developed by Vietnamese developer Dong Nguyen and released in 2013. In the game, players control a bird and navigate it through a series of pipes by tapping the screen to keep the bird flying.

How do you play Flappy Bird?

To play Flappy Bird, simply tap the screen to make the bird flap its wings and ascend. The objective is to navigate the bird through gaps between green pipes without colliding with them. Each successful pass through a pair of pipes earns the player one point.

Why is Flappy Bird so difficult?

Flappy Bird's difficulty lies in its simple yet challenging gameplay mechanics. Players must have precise timing and coordination to navigate the bird through the narrow gaps between pipes. The speed of the game also gradually increases, making it more challenging as players progress.

How can I improve at Flappy Bird?

Improving at Flappy Bird requires practice and patience. Focus on timing your taps to keep the bird at the right height to pass through the pipes. It's essential to stay calm and avoid overreacting to obstacles. Additionally, studying the movement patterns of the pipes can help anticipate upcoming obstacles.

Are there any cheats or hacks for Flappy Bird?

While there may be cheats or hacks available for Flappy Bird, it's essential to note that using them can detract from the challenge and satisfaction of the game. Additionally, some cheats or hacks may violate the game's terms of service or be considered cheating by other players.

Can I still download Flappy Bird?

Flappy Bird was removed from app stores by its developer in 2014 due to its addictive nature and the overwhelming attention it received. However, it is still possible to find the game available for download through unofficial sources or by purchasing a device with the game pre-installed.

Is Flappy Bird available on all devices?

The key features of Flappy Bird

Simple Gameplay

Flappy Bird is known for its straightforward yet challenging gameplay. Players control a bird by tapping the screen to keep it airborne and navigate it through gaps between pipes.

Minimalist Graphics

The game features simple, retro-style graphics with a minimalistic design. The background is often a plain, blue sky, and the obstacles consist of green pipes.

High Difficulty

Flappy Bird is notoriously difficult. The narrow gaps between pipes and the increasing speed of the game make it challenging to navigate the bird without colliding with obstacles.

Score Tracking

The game keeps track of the player's score, displayed at the top of the screen. Each successful pass through a pair of pipes earns the player one point.

Instant Restart:

When the player collides with an obstacle and the game ends, they can quickly restart by tapping the screen, allowing for rapid retries.

Global Popularity

Flappy Bird gained immense popularity worldwide shortly after its release in 2013, becoming one of the most downloaded and talked-about mobile games at the time.
